About Us

SPELL is a Solar Powered Educational Learning Library: a digital library over an off-line WiFi hotspot, designed to simulate an online experience. Making use of open educational resources and ever-smaller and more efficient technology, SPELL provides an all-in-one, self-powered plug-and-play kit, ready to be deployed with absolute minimal training or maintenance required for start-up and continued operation.

About ASU Libraries

Arizona State University Libraries, among the top 40 research libraries in North America, is recognized for the strength of its collections. Included with the physical library locations is an extensive online repository of resources and research tools, including the ASU's catalog as well as links to hundreds of online databases and catalogs of library materials.
